How commissions work

A straightforward process.

  1. Get in touch

    Email or call with what you have in mind — a species, a subject, a size, a budget range if you have one.

  2. Discuss the brief

    Carl will talk through what's possible, in wood, pewter, bronze, resin, or taxidermy, and give you a realistic timeframe.

  3. Commission the piece

    Once agreed, the piece is made by hand — the same process behind work now held in museums and private collections worldwide.
